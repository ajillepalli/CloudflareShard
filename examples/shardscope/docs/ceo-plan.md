## Vision

### Premise challenge (verdict: wedge is correct, do not change)
"Watch a live cluster reshard under load with zero lost writes" is the right
spearpoint: it attacks the incumbent's crown jewel (Vitess/PlanetScale online
resharding), it's the hardest claim to fake, and it's the most visual. 2PC,
indexes, and multi-tenancy are table stakes — correctly relegated to supporting
rooms, not the lead.

### 10x check
The 10-star is not a different wedge. It's making THIS wedge adversarially
credible, emotionally felt, and personally ownable:
- **Credible:** let the viewer try to break it (chaos mode) → asserted correctness
  becomes earned correctness.
- **Felt:** contrast the incumbent's pain (maintenance window, rejected writes) so
  `lost: 0` lands as relief, not a number.
- **Ownable:** one-click "deploy your own" closes the watch→have gap that otherwise
  kills developer conversion.

### Platonic ideal (12 months)
Shardscope is the link people paste to argue edge-native databases can do what
Vitess does. Someone tweets "I tried for 10 minutes to make it lose a write during
a reshard and couldn't," with a clip. A developer clicks "deploy my own," has a
cluster in 60s, never opens a docs page. The reshard-under-load proof is the spine;
every addition makes it credible, felt, or ownable.

### Inversion (what makes it fail)
(a) Reads as scripted/fake → mitigated by chaos mode + real correctness meter.
(b) Viewers don't feel why resharding matters → mitigated by incumbent contrast.
(c) Beautiful but nobody can run it → mitigated by deploy-your-own.

## Accepted Scope (added to this plan)
- Phase 1: chaos "break it" control panel on top of the correctness core — real
  attacks (kill shard mid-migration, drain hot node, double-submit, mismatched
  idempotent replay, blip shard during cutover); the meter must survive all, and
  each attack needs a test proving the meter catches a *genuine* loss.
- Phase 2: incumbent-contrast framing beat (maintenance-window resharding vs live
  cutover). Copy must be accurate/fair, not a strawman.
- Phase 3: global edge latency map (multi-region origination, real or credibly
  simulated); one-click Deploy-to-Cloudflare (deploy template + account OAuth +
  cost/rate-limit/teardown).
